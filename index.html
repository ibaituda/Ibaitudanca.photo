About me

Telling the story of football
through photography.

I started photography in Norway, drawn first to landscapes, northern lights and the discipline of waiting for the right moment. That same instinct eventually brought me pitchside, where football quickly became the place where my way of seeing made the most sense.

Today I work as a sports photographer covering professional football and elite competitions, including LaLiga, UEFA Champions League, Copa del Rey and Eliteserien. My work is focused on clubs, players, agencies and editorial distribution, with images created for press, social media, communication departments and international platforms.

I look for photographs that feel direct, clean and useful: decisive actions, emotional reactions, portraits of competition and moments that explain the game without needing too many words. For me, the job is not only taking the picture, but delivering it quickly, consistently and with the standard required while the story is still alive.
